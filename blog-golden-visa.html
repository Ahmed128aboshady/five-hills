How to Secure the Dubai Golden Visa via Property

A comprehensive, step-by-step guide for international property investors in the UAE

Dubai’s real estate market continues to attract global investors, not only for tax-free yields but also for the long-term residency benefits. The UAE Golden Visa stands out as a highly sought-after program, offering foreign nationals a 10-year self-sponsored residency. If you are looking to secure a Golden Visa, real estate is one of the most straightforward pathways. In this detailed guide, we explore the exact investment criteria, the application process, and how you can leverage 5 Hills' premium portfolio to qualify.

1. The Core Investment Criteria (AED 2M+)

To qualify for the 10-year residency visa via real estate, you must meet the following financial requirements established by the Dubai Land Department (DLD):

  • Minimum Value: You must purchase property with a gross purchase value of at least AED 2,000,000 (Two Million UAE Dirhams). This is roughly equivalent to $544,500 USD.
  • Portfolio Consolidation: The investment does not have to be in a single property. You can combine multiple properties (e.g., buying two apartments worth AED 1 Million each) to reach the required threshold.
  • Mortgaged Properties: If the property is mortgaged, the bank must issue a No Objection Certificate (NOC) to the Land Department. Crucially, the investor must have paid a minimum of AED 2 Million directly as a down payment or principal repayment.
  • Joint Ownership: Spouses can jointly own a single property worth AED 2 Million to qualify. For non-spouses, each individual's share in the title deed must be valued at a minimum of AED 2 Million.

2. Ready vs. Off-Plan Property Eligibility

In the past, the Golden Visa required the property to be fully completed. Under current regulations, both **Ready** and **Off-Plan** assets qualify:

  • Ready Properties: Purchasing a ready-to-move-in property, such as our luxury apartments at 1 Residences in Wasl 1 or Cedarwood Estates, allows you to apply for the Golden Visa immediately upon receiving the Title Deed.
  • Off-Plan Properties: Investors can also qualify by purchasing off-plan projects from approved developers. The property must be worth AED 2M+, and the developer must be registered with RERA with an active escrow account. An excellent example from our portfolio is Knightsbridge Phase 1, offering premium English-style smart villas in Meydan starting from AED 8.5 Million.

3. Step-by-Step Application Process

Securing the Golden Visa involves several administrative steps. Working with a registered agency like 5 Hills simplifies this process:

  1. Select and Purchase: Choose an eligible property from our portfolio. If buying off-plan, we arrange the sales agreement (SPA) and register it with the developer. If ready, we handle the transfer of ownership at a DLD trustee office.
  2. Apply for DLD Approval: Submit the Title Deed, passport copy, current visa page, and a clean police clearance certificate to the Dubai Land Department.
  3. Medical Fitness Test: Once initial approval is granted, you must undergo a standard medical fitness test (blood test and chest X-ray) at an authorized medical center in Dubai.
  4. Biometrics and Emirates ID: Complete your biometric scanning (fingerprints) at an ICP center.
  5. Visa Issuance: Upon successful screening, the 10-year residency visa is stamped digitally. Your new physical Emirates ID will be delivered shortly after.

4. Key Benefits of the Dubai Golden Visa

Beyond residency, the Golden Visa unlocks exceptional privileges for you and your family:

  • 10-Year Security: Enjoy a long-term residency that is fully renewable upon expiration, as long as you maintain ownership of the qualifying asset.
  • Family Sponsorship: Sponsor your spouse, children of any age, and parents. You can also sponsor unlimited domestic staff.
  • No Minimum Stay: Unlike standard visas, there is no requirement to visit Dubai every 6 months. You can reside abroad and retain your residency.
  • Esaad Privilege Card: Golden Visa holders receive the exclusive Esaad card, offering significant discounts on retail, education, healthcare, and hospitality across the UAE.
